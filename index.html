About Me

Hello! My name is Ashish Kumar Jha, and I have a deep passion for building scalable, data-driven solutions. My journey into data engineering started when I automated my first ETL pipeline, optimizing data delivery and reducing processing time significantly. That experience sparked my fascination with cloud computing, big data, and real-time analytics.
